## 7. YOUR RIGHTS UNDER GDPR

### 7.1 Right of Access (Article 15)
- Request copies of your personal data
- Information about processing purposes and legal basis
- Contact: privacy@mdmcmusicads.com

### 7.2 Right to Rectification (Article 16)
- Correct inaccurate personal data
- Complete incomplete data
- Self-service available in account settings

### 7.3 Right to Erasure (Article 17)
- Request deletion of personal data
- Exceptions: Legal obligations, legitimate interests
- Processing time: 30 days maximum

### 7.4 Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)
- Temporarily limit data processing
- Available during dispute resolution
- Data marked as restricted, not deleted

### 7.5 Right to Data Portability (Article 20)
- Receive data in machine-readable format
- Transfer data to another controller
- Available for contract and consent-based processing

### 7.6 Right to Object (Article 21)
- Object to legitimate interest processing
- Absolute right for direct marketing
- Balancing test may apply

### 7.7 Right to Withdraw Consent (Article 7(3))
- Withdraw consent at any time
- Does not affect past processing
- Use consent management interface

### 7.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint (Article 77)
- Contact your national supervisory authority
- France: CNIL (https://www.cnil.fr/)
- EU: https://edpb.europa.eu/about-edpb/board/members_en

**Rights Exercise:** Email privacy@mdmcmusicads.com with proof of identity. Response within 30 days.
